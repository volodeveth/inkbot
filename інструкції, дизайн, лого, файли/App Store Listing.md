## Full Description

# Transform Your Product Listings with AI-Powered Descriptions

InkBot uses advanced AI to generate compelling, SEO-optimized product descriptions that convert browsers into buyers. Save hours of writing time and boost your store's visibility.

## Why Choose InkBot?

**⚡ Instant Generation**
Create professional product descriptions in seconds, not hours. Simply enter your product details and let AI do the heavy lifting.

**🎯 SEO Optimized**
Every description comes with:
- SEO score (0-100) with real-time feedback
- Meta title and description tags
- Recommended keywords for better rankings

**🌍 42 Languages Supported**
Reach global customers with descriptions in English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Ukrainian, and 35+ more languages.

**🏪 9 Industry Niches**
Tailored prompts for your specific industry:
- Fashion & Apparel
- Electronics & Tech
- Beauty & Cosmetics
- Food & Beverages
- Home & Garden
- Sports & Fitness
- Jewelry & Accessories
- Pets & Animals
- General Products

**🎨 6 Writing Tones**
Match your brand voice: Professional, Casual, Luxurious, Playful, Technical, or Minimalist.

**📝 Brand Voice Customization**
Configure your unique brand voice with custom keywords, phrases to avoid, target audience, and sample texts. InkBot learns your style.

**📦 Bulk Generation**
Process multiple products at once. Perfect for large catalogs or seasonal updates.

**📊 History & Analytics**
Track all your generations, view SEO scores, and reuse successful descriptions.

**🔌 API Access (Elite Plan)**
Integrate InkBot into your workflow with our REST API. Generate descriptions programmatically.

## Pricing

- **Free**: 5 generations/month — Perfect for trying out
- **Starter ($9/mo)**: 100 generations/month — For growing stores
- **Pro ($19/mo)**: 500 generations/month — For active sellers
- **Elite ($49/mo)**: 5,000 generations/month + API access — For power users

## How It Works

1. **Select a product** from your store or enter details manually
2. **Choose your settings** — niche, tone, language
3. **Generate** — AI creates your description with SEO tags
4. **Apply** — One click to update your Shopify product
