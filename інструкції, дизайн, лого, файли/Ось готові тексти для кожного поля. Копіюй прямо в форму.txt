  App details (0/500)

  InkBot helps merchants create high-quality product descriptions without manual writing. Select a product from your
  store, choose an industry niche and writing tone, and receive a complete description with meta tags and SEO score in
  seconds. The app supports 111 languages for global selling. Brand voice settings let you train the AI to match your
  unique style with custom keywords, target audience, and sample texts. Bulk generation processes multiple products at
  once, saving time for large catalogs. All generations are saved in history for easy reuse.

  Testing instructions:
  To test this app:
  1. Install the app on a development store
  2. Open the app from Shopify admin sidebar
  3. Go to Generate page, enter any product title (e.g. "Premium Wireless Headphones")
  4. Select a niche (e.g. Electronics), tone (e.g. Professional), and language
  5. Click Generate and wait for the AI description with SEO score
  6. Try Bulk Generate to process multiple products from your catalog
  7. Visit Settings to configure Brand Voice preferences
  8. Check History to see all past generations
